Bringing e-commerce power to the physical aisle.

For Shoppers

Elevate the Shopper Experience

Turn every trip into a seamless, high-tech journey.

  • Intelligent Wayfinding: Shoppers search for items on the HD touchscreen and get real-time, interactive, GPS-style maps directly to the shelf.
  • Digital List Syncing: Customers scan a QR code to sync their shopping list; items automatically check off as they are placed in the basket.
  • Frictionless Savings: Personalized digital coupons and loyalty points apply automatically, showing a transparent, rolling subtotal to eliminate checkout anxiety.
  • On-Demand Product Insights: Tapping an item reveals ingredient profiles, allergen warnings, and smart pairing recommendations (e.g., suggesting a high-margin wine for steak).
  • Premium Convenience: Integrated USB charging ports keep mobile devices powered, while electric-assist motors make heavy carts effortless to push.
For Retailers & CPG

Unlock the Retailer & CPG Advantage

Transform standard shopping carts into your most profitable digital assets.

1

High-Margin Retail Media Networks (RMN)

Monetize your on-cart screens. CPG brands pay premium rates to display hyper-targeted ads at the exact point of decision — yielding unmatched conversion rates and 70–90% digital profit margins.

2

Strategic Basket Expansion

Drive larger transaction sizes automatically. Software-driven gamification, loyalty progress bars, and machine-learning upsells encourage shoppers to add "one more item" to hit discount thresholds.

3

"In-Store Clickstream" Analytics

Gain complete visibility inside the aisles. Track shopper navigation heatmaps, capture valuable "pick-up/put-back" behavioral data, and run real-time A/B tests on digital ad creatives.

4

Operational Efficiency & Asset Protection

Integrate hardware intelligence with your POS. Update inventory logs in real-time as items are carted, and protect your investment with built-in GPS and geo-fenced asset tracking.

5

Exponential Loyalty Adoption

Increase digital loyalty engagement by 40% or more. By making loyalty login the "hook" to access premium features (like frictionless checkout), you build a continuous, clean data loop for offline-to-online marketing.
