AI Storage Systems

AI-driven storage solutions designed for machine learning and data-intensive workloads. Ethersoft dynamically adapts to data type and performance demands, balancing speed and cost in real time.

  • Performance Management — automatically adjusts storage class and throughput for AI workloads.
  • Data-Type Intelligence — classifies and routes structured, unstructured, and semi-structured data.
  • AI-Aware Governance — integrates compliance and access controls into the data pipeline.
  • Cross-Cloud Cost Optimization — moves underutilized data to lower-cost classes without disruption.
  • Unified Cloud Visibility — end-to-end view of usage, performance, and spend across providers.
  • Smart Data Placement — AI policies store each dataset in the right tier by access, priority, and compliance.

Storage Optimizator

An intelligent optimization layer for multi-cloud environments. Ethersoft automatically analyzes usage across AWS, GCP, and Azure to place data in the most cost-efficient storage tiers.

🗄️

GCNV Storage Manager

A powerful management platform for Google Cloud NetApp Volumes (GCNV). It unifies monitoring, optimization, and automation in one control plane.

  • Monitoring — unified view of performance, utilization, and capacity from a single dashboard.
  • Optimization — proprietary algorithm analyzes usage in real time and applies cost-saving actions.
  • QoS Management — adjust Quality of Service per volume for consistent critical-workload performance.
  • Alerts — proactive capacity alerts before volumes fill, preventing downtime.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many Google Cloud projects can I manage with Ethersoft?

Our pricing is per TB, so there is no limit on how many projects you can manage.

How does Ethersoft integrate with my existing Google Cloud setup?

Ethersoft integrates seamlessly through Google Cloud APIs. Setup takes less than 10 minutes and requires no changes to your existing infrastructure, with service-account authentication and IAM-based permissions.

Do you offer custom integrations or enterprise features?

Yes. Our Enterprise plan includes custom integrations, on-premise deployment options, and dedicated support. Contact us to discuss your requirements and custom SLA needs.

Is my data secure with Ethersoft?

Yes. We follow industry-standard security practices, use read-only API access where possible, never store sensitive data permanently, encrypt all communications, and maintain SOC 2 compliance.

What kind of cost savings can I expect?

Customers typically see a 20–40% reduction in NetApp storage costs through right-sizing recommendations and usage optimization. Exact savings depend on your current utilization patterns.
