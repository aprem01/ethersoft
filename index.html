How It Works

From connection to continuous optimization

A deliberately short path from first call to measurable results.

Connect

Integrate through secure cloud APIs in under ten minutes — service-account authentication, IAM-based permissions, no infrastructure changes.

Analyze

Ethersoft maps your data, usage patterns, and spend across AWS, GCP, and Azure to surface where performance and cost are leaking.

Optimize

Our algorithms right-size storage, place data in the correct tiers, and apply cost-saving actions automatically — with you in control.

Sustain

Continuous monitoring and proactive alerts keep your environment efficient and stable, preventing outages before they happen.
