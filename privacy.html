Privacy Policy

How Ethersoft Inc. collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: 2026

Overview

Ethersoft Inc. ("Ethersoft," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website and services, how we use it, and the choices available to you. By using our website you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

  • Information you provide — when you complete a contact form or email us, we collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, and the contents of your message.
  • Service data — where you use our products, we access cloud-environment metadata through authorized, least-privilege API connections. We use read-only access where possible and do not store sensitive customer data permanently.
  • Usage information — basic, aggregated analytics about how visitors use our website (such as pages viewed), used only to improve the site.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide the services you request.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our products and website.
  • To communicate with you about your account, requests, or relevant updates.
  • To meet legal, security, and compliance obligations.

Data Security

We follow industry-standard security practices, encrypt communications in transit, apply least-privilege access controls, and maintain SOC 2–aligned practices. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information using appropriate safeguards.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us operate (under confidentiality obligations), or where required by law.

Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. You can also opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

Contact
